How Fire Weather Index Works

The FWI combines four weather factors to estimate forest fire danger:

  • Temperature: Higher temperatures dry out vegetation, increasing fire risk
  • Humidity: Low humidity means drier air and fuel, raising fire probability
  • Wind Speed: Strong winds spread fires faster and supply oxygen
  • Precipitation: Recent rain reduces fire risk by moistening fuels
